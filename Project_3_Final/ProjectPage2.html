AIRESS

Logo and Packaging Design

Airess is a fictional company that sells all-natural sparkling juice. We created a name, logo, and branding system based on a design brief about the company and its goals.

Airess was created in response to the company’s interest in a logo that expressed the healthy natual quality of their drinks while incorporating some type of history or mythology. Airmid is the Celtic goddes of health, always pictured with flowing hair filled with items from nature around her. As the name Airmid doesn’t mean anything and could be confusing, we changed it to Airess: the “air” references the air in the sparkling juice, while airess sounds more explictly like a godess. The waves of her hair contain the bubbles from the soda and become a theme throughout the entire branding system.
