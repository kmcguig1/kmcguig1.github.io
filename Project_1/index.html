Tenez

// Bold Italic

Tenez is a display serif font family designed by Brazilian Rodrigo Saiani and published by Plau. The didone typeface was designed for a branding project for Wimbledon, and is a display face meant to be large and make an impact. It takes inspiration from nib calligraphy and typefaces like Didot and Bodoni. The typewriter-reminiscent stroke contrast, wavy baseline, round stroke endings, and beautiful curves give it a unique elegance and charm.
